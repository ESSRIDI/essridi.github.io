About Me

I recently took a major step forward to change my professional path. I moved from structural steel Draughtsman with more than 6 years’ experience to become Web & Mobile Application Developer (Full Stack). To reach my goal, each day for me is a new challenge, a new adventure to deal with.

I taught myself to be patient and wait for the right moment to make the right decision. For many years and in my previous career experiences, I was interested in automating tasks and making things more dynamic.

Pursuing this way, I have learned many programing languages’ like C#, PHP , JavaScript, working with frameworks like Symfony…and more ,also libraries like ReactJs, Twig and APIs’ of various software’s like Tekla openApi, AutoCAD .NET API…and so on. Having worked on many work environments, today, I have several skills that allow me to bring a real plus to companies. That's why, I’m always looking forward to new challenges.
